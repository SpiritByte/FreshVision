About Us

FreshVision, established in 2023, is a company founded by four high school students dedicated to reducing food waste to achieve SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production. Using our special anti-freeze cameras, we take photos of your fresh produce and notify you when it ripens so that you can use your food effectively and don’t let it go to waste. We also suggest recipes you can use so that you don’t worry about what to cook!
